Frequently Asked Questions

Is Meetz free to use?

Yes, Meetz is completely free. No credit card, no subscription, no hidden charges. Just enter a nickname and start talking.

Do I need to create an account?

No account needed. Just type a nickname and you're in. Your nickname is saved in your browser so you don't need to enter it again.

Is Meetz safe?

Meetz has built-in report and block features. All connections are anonymous. We don't store any conversation data. Users must be 18 or older.

What devices does Meetz work on?

Meetz works on any device with a modern browser — desktop, laptop, tablet, or mobile. No app download required. Works on Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge.

What is Connect Code?

Connect Code is a unique feature on Meetz. Share a secret code word with a friend — when both of you enter the same code, you get matched directly to each other instead of a random stranger.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Information We Collect

Meetz does not require account registration. We collect minimal information: a nickname you choose voluntarily (stored only in your browser), your approximate country (detected via IP for matching purposes only), and standard server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamps) for security and abuse prevention.

2. How We Use Information

Your nickname and country are used solely to match you with other users. We do not sell, share, or trade any personal information with third parties. Server logs are retained for up to 30 days for security purposes only.

3. Cookies and Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings. We also use localStorage to save your nickname and friends list on your device only.

4. Video and Audio

Video and audio during chats are transmitted directly between users via WebRTC peer-to-peer technology. Meetz does not record, store, or have access to any video or audio conversations.

5. Age Requirement

Meetz is strictly for users aged 18 and above. By using this platform you confirm you are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

6. Data Security

All connections to Meetz are encrypted via HTTPS. We implement reasonable security measures to protect against unauthorised access.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: privacy@meetz.onrender.com
